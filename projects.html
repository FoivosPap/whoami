Projects

Project Description
Swarm aggregation of robotic vehicles Eight robotic vehicles that form a swarm are simulated in Matlab. A controller is designed so that the equilibrium of the system is Lyapunov stable.
Estimating F matrix of two images A corner detection algorithm is used to estimate the corners of an object in two pictures of the same object taken from different angles. The software is developed in Matlab.
Motion simulation and trajectory planning of a 4 DOF RRRP robotic arm The forward and inverse kinematics of a 4 DOF RRRP robotic arm are calculated. A circular trajectory of the end effector is planned and simulated in Matlab. A feedforward torque controller is designed.
Localisation of small robotic vehicles (amigobots) in a known environment The centre and orientation of the frame surrounding the robotic vehicle is calculated with the least squares method using the sonar measurements. The trajectory of the vehicle is then estimated and compared with the data from the encoders. The software is developed in Matlab.
Teleoparation and motion control of a 3 DOF RRR robotic arm (LARrobot) The software is developed in Matlab.
Mesh simplification algorithm based on edge collapse The software is developed in C++.
